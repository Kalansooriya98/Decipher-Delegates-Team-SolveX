About Our Case

Business Situation

Dundee Precious Metals is a Canadian based, international gold mining company. Their operations include the Chelopech underground gold mine in Bulgaria and the Tsumeb smelter, a complex copper concentrate processing facility in Namibia. DPM holds interests in developing gold and exploration properties located in Bulgaria, including the Krumovgrad gold project, expected to commence production early in 2019, Canada and Serbia; and a 10.2% interest in Sabina Gold & Silver Corp.Their business strategy is focused on: Operation efficiency and accountability, Core business excellence, Corporate responsibility, Creativity and innovation which are key to achieving their goals and generating long-term value for their stakeholders.

Main Problem

The main problem of this case is that the price of gold and other metals is fluctuating wildly

Solution

As a solution for this condition Dundee was thought to lower the gold price by making its mining operations more efficient. In order to do that Management has decided to implement an underground wireless Wi-Fi network that allows electronic devices to exchange data wirelessly at the Chelopech mine to monitor the location of equipment, people, and ore throughout the mineâ€™s tunnels and facilities.
